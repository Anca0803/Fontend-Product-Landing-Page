Elegant shoes made of the best quality

Best leather quality

Leather is the most important material in shoe production for us at Merkis. In order to meet our quality standards, we use only the highest-quality materials and produce our shoes exclusively in Europe.

Leather processing

All of our decisions are based on sustainability and carefulness. Our tanneries in Italy and Germany adhere to the strictest environmental regulations. In this respect, it is particularly important to us, due to the fact that the tanning process calls for the use of water, that this water is free from toxic substances or other harmful chemicals when it is discharged into the ecosystem.

Lining materials

At Merkis, everything that comes into direct contact with the skin must be breathable and free from harmful substances. We therefore primarily use chrome-free tanned leather as a lining material.
